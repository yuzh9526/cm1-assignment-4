This is my Iceland Travel!

Last year, I took a nine-day trip to Iceland. I saw a lot of beautiful scenery and took a lot of good pictures. Here, I hope to be able to share with you these beautiful scenery.

Along the way, I saw too many beautiful scenery. I was shocked by nature. I saw beautiful icebergs, pure white snow and ice, and colorful auroras. This is the first aurora I've seen, but I hope it won't be the last. When I walk on the glacier, I can feel its firmness and transparency. When I saw diamond beach and black beach, I was filled with excitement.
